- When walking at speed s + 2 km/h, total time is 2 hours 24 minutes (i.e., 2.4 hours).
- The coffee shop time t is constant in both cases.

- Walking time = distance / speed.

- Once s and t are known, total time at speed s + 0.5 can be computed as (9 / (s + 0.5)) + (t / 60).
