For each element a_i in A, the sets B can include any subset of the elements from A that are less than or equal to a_i, and must include a_i itself. The number of nonempty subsets of the elements less than or equal to a_i is 2^(i-1) (since there are i-1 elements less than a_i, and each can either be included or not).
