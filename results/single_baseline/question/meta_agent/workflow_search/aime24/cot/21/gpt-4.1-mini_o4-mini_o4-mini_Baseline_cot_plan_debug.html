A rectangle arises by choosing two distinct lines of one orientation and two of a perpendicular orientation.  In a regular 12-gon, the 66 lines fall into 12 orientation‐classes (indexed m=0,…,11), where class m has C_m = 5 lines if m is even and C_m = 6 if m is odd.
