We set up a coordinate system with the sphere of radius 11 centered at the origin and the torus of tube‐radius a=3 and center‐circle radius R=6 whose axis is the z‐axis.  Shifting the torus upward by H, its surface is given in cylindrical coords (ρ,z) by (ρ−6)^2+(z−H)^2=9, while the sphere is ρ^2+z^2=121.
