We need a list of positive integers summing to 30, with unique mode 9 and median (an integer) not appearing in the list. If the list has odd length, the median is an actual list element, so must be even length. Try length 4: let the sorted list be [a1,a2,a3,a4]; a3=a4=9 to make 9 the unique mode.
