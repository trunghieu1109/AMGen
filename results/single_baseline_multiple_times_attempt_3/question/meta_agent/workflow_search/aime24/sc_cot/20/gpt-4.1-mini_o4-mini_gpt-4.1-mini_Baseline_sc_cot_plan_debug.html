We seek positive integers n with two digits in base b (so n=A b+B with 1≤A≤b−1, 0≤B≤b−1) and A+B=√n.  Set s=A+B so n=s² and A b+B=s² ⇒ A(b−1)=s(s−1).  Writing d=b−1, we need s(s−1) divisible by d and A=s(s−1)/d≤d, B=s−A≥0.
