Set up equations:
Walking time at speed s = 9/s hours = 540/s minutes
Total time at speed s = walking time + t = 540/s + t = 240

Walking time at speed s+2 = 9/(s+2) hours = 540/(s+2) minutes
Total time at speed s+2 = walking time + t = 540/(s+2) + t = 144

Subtract the second equation from the first:
(540/s + t) - (540/(s+2) + t) = 240 - 144
540/s - 540/(s+2) = 96

540s + 1080 - 540s = 96 s^2 + 192 s
