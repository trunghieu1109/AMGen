Let s be Aya’s walking speed. At speed s: walking time = 9/s hours = (540/s) minutes, plus t minutes shop time equals total 240 minutes. At speed s+2: walking time = 9/(s+2) hours = (540/(s+2)) minutes, plus t = 144 minutes.
