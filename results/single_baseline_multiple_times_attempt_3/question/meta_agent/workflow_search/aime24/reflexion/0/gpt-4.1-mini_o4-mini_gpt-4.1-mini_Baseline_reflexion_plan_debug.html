Let s be Aya’s walking speed (km/h) and t the coffee time (minutes).  
1. When she walks at speed s: total time = walking time + coffee time = 9/s hours + t/60 hours = 4 hours.  
2. When she walks at speed s+2: total time = 9/(s+2) + t/60 = 2.4 hours.
