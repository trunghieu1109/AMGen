Let the grid entries be a,b,c in the first row and d,e,f in the second. Reading rows gives numbers 100a+10b+c and 100d+10e+f, whose sum is 999, so 100(a+d)+10(b+e)+(c+f)=999 ⇒ a+d=9, b+e=9, c+f=9.
